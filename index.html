How We Work

Step 1: Patient Registration

Patients register on our platform, providing essential information to get matched with the right therapist.

Step 2: Therapy Plan Creation

Therapists create a personalized therapy plan with goals and activities tailored to each patient’s needs.

Step 3: Session Scheduling

Therapy sessions are scheduled and patients receive reminders, with options for in-person or online sessions.

Step 4: Progress Review

Supervisors review progress reports after each session and provide feedback, ensuring continuous improvement.

About Us

At SpeechGuide, we are dedicated to transforming the way Speech-Language Therapy services are delivered. Our mission is to provide an innovative, digital-first platform that empowers patients, therapists, and supervisors to collaborate seamlessly. With a deep understanding of the challenges faced in clinical settings, especially the growing case load and limited supervision resources, we’ve developed a solution that streamlines the entire therapy process.

From case allocation to therapy plan documentation, progress tracking, and teletherapy support, our platform ensures that therapy is both accessible and effective. We are committed to enhancing the quality of care, providing tools that improve communication, and fostering an environment where patients can reach their full potential.

Join us on our mission to make speech therapy more efficient, connected, and results-driven!
